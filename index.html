FAQ

Why another donating platform?
We believe donating money to people in need should be easy, simple and straightforward. Lots of people who would like to help often fail to do so due to difficult or slow process of making an actual donation. We believe a donation should be only a click away.
What's wrong with calling a fundrasing line?
Calling a fundraising telephone number is fine but usually operators take a cut so that actual donated amout is smaller. We believe making donations online could increase the net donated amount significantly.
Why not just pay directly?
Please do! The goal of online fundraising platform is to enable one-click donations for those people that would like to donate but are sometimes discouraged by banking payment process. With an application directly available on your smartphone you can pay quickly and hassle free within seconds.
How does it work for fundraisers?
Imagine a place where you could describe the fundraising cause and share that mission with all good people willing to help in matter of minutes. By means of online fundraising platform you could raise additional funding by reaching out to people who are willing to help but would like a quick and hassle free way of donating directly from their smartphone.
How does it work for donators?
Open fundraising app on your smarphone, select the fundraising campaign and with one-click donate the money to people in need, it's that simple.
